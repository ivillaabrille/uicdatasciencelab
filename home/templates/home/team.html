The Lab and The Team

The Purpose and Background of the Lab

To develop research projects specifically in the field of data sciences to further enhance and expand the knowledge of the students about Data Science. It also helps the students to have more knowledge thus opening lots of opportunities for the students because of the chance that they can present their studies nationally and internationally and gather fundings for their respective Capstone projects. This is the first and currently the only Data Analytics Lab in Mindanao.


Current Projects Being Developed

HALANG: This study is about animal species specifically the endemic birds here in Mindanao and create datasets about them to help researchers who are currently studying or those researchers who are going to study about the animals and to provide the legitimacy of the data the data will be provided and inputted by DENR to the System.

TANOM: This study is about plants species specifically the endemic birds here in Mindanao and create datasets about them to help researchers who are currently studying or those researchers who are going to study about the animals and to provide the legitimacy of the data the data will be provided and inputted by DENR to the System.


Resources Currently Available

Arduino kits, Raspberry Pi, 5 computer units, TV, Projector, White Glassboard, all inside an airconditioned room.
